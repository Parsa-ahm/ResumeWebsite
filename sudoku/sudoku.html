Sudoku C++

A Sudoku solver built with C++ and compiled to WebAssembly for high-performance solving in the browser. Input your Sudoku puzzle or generate a new one, then click "Solve" to see the solution. The solver uses backtracking algorithms to efficiently find solutions to any valid Sudoku puzzle.
